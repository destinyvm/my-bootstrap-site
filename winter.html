Winter solstice!

The winter solstice is the reversal of the Sun's apparent ebbing in the sky.

The daytime stops becoming shorter and begins to lengthen again.

In parts of ancient Europe, this was symbolized as the death and rebirth of the Sun,

or of a Sun god.
