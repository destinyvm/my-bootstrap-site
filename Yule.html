Yule Lads!

The story goes that if the child has been well behaved,

a Yule Lad will leave candy or a present of some sort.

If the child was misbehaved, they could expect to wake to a shoe full of rotten potatoes.

In addition to their various gifts, the Yule Lads were also known to be clever pranksters.
